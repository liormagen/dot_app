Privacy Policy

Dot Story  ·  Last updated: May 7, 2026

Short version: Dot Story does not collect, store, or share any personal information. It works entirely offline and contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Who we are

Dot Story is a connect-the-dots drawing app for children, developed by Lior Magen. You can reach us at cijalm@gmail.com.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically, Dot Story:

  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Does not use analytics or crash-reporting services
  • Does not contain advertising of any kind
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local storage

Dot Story saves your progress (completed drawings, settings, and whether you have unlocked the full version) locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

In-app purchases

The optional "Unlock Full Access" purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Apple's purchase records are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Dot Story is designed for children ages 4–14. Because we collect no data at all, there is nothing special we need to do to protect children's privacy — there is simply no data to protect. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's Kids Category guidelines.

Third-party SDKs and services

Dot Story uses no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or social media integrations. All fonts are bundled inside the app and no network requests are made during normal use.

Network access

Dot Story does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests during normal gameplay. The only network activity is the one-time In-App Purchase transaction handled by Apple.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Because we collect no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes to this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at cijalm@gmail.com.
